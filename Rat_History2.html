Soldiers hated rats but sometimes they were helpful. They could sense an oncoming attack which warned the soldiers about their enemies.

In Belgium they are training rats to search for people. They are training them to be rescue rats. Rats are often adopted out in groups because they are very social.

Brown rats are often used as lab rats. They are bred and kept for scientific research. They are closely related to the human genome. They play an important role in biomedical research. They are widely used in fields of neuroscience, physiology, toxicology, immunology, and cardiovascular, and behavioral studies.
