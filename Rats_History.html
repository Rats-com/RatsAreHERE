Rats have been kept as pets in various countries for thousands of years but have become domesticated in Europe these last 200 years.

The fancy rat descends from a brown rat not the black rat that carried the plague. The brown rat came in and pushed the black rat out.

Rats that were albino or different color were kept and bred. Brown rats made their way to America on ships.
